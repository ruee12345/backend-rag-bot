HR Leave Policy

1. Annual Leave
   - Employees are entitled to 15 days of annual leave per year.
   - Leave accrues monthly at a rate of 1.25 days.
   - Minimum 7 days notice required for planned leave.

2. Sick Leave
   - 10 days of paid sick leave per year.
   - Medical certificate required for more than 3 consecutive days.
   - Can be taken as half days.

3. Maternity Leave
   - 26 weeks of paid maternity leave.
   - Application must be submitted 8 weeks before due date.
   - Additional 4 weeks unpaid leave available.

4. Paternity Leave
   - 2 weeks of paid paternity leave.
   - Must be taken within 6 months of child's birth.

5. Leave Approval
   - All leaves require manager approval.
   - Submit request through HR portal.
   - Emergency leave: Notify within 24 hours.
